### **Artificial Intelligence**  
**Sep 2023 - Present | Xi'an Jiaotong University(Integrated Master–PhD Program)**  

My research focuses on perception and multi-modal intelligence for robotics and autonomous driving, including LiDAR-based SLAM, BEV perception, and Vision-Language Models (VLM) for UAV scenarios.

#### Research Projects:  
- **LiDAR-based SLAM**:
  1. Developed a LiDAR-based Simultaneous Localization and Mapping (SLAM) system for robust localization and real-time mapping in dynamic environments;
  2. Improved system robustness and efficiency for reliable performance in complex scenarios.

- **BEV Perception for Autonomous Driving**:
  1. Built a Bird’s-Eye-View (BEV) perception framework supporting multi-camera and/or LiDAR inputs;
  2. Implemented view transformation from image space to BEV space and designed multi-sensor feature fusion methods;
  3. Developed detection and segmentation heads for 3D object detection and map element prediction in BEV space.

- **UAV Vision-Language Navigation**:
  1. Designed a Vision-Language Model (VLM) for UAV scenarios to enable multi-modal perception and semantic reasoning;
  2. Integrated visual features with language representations for cross-modal understanding and instruction following.
